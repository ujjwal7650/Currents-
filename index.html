About Currents

who's behind this, and how it works

Currents is a lightweight market dashboard built for one job: showing where forex and crypto rates stand right now, and how they got there. No account, no ads, no paywall — just live numbers pulled straight from public market data on every page load.

Everything updates automatically every 60 seconds. The converter, the FX and crypto grids, and the historical chart all share the same live rate table, so a number you see in one place always matches the others.

  • Forex dataFrankfurter (European Central Bank)
  • Crypto dataCoinGecko
  • Refresh interval60 seconds
  • Historical rangeup to 1 year
Rates are sourced from third-party public APIs and may lag live exchange pricing by a few minutes. Currents is an informational tool, not financial advice — verify rates with your bank or exchange before making a transaction.
